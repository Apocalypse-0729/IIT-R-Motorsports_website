BOLT

  • With the automotive industry shifting towards electric vehicles and increasing climate change concerns, we developed an all-electric vehicle - BOLT in 2015 and participated in FSAE Australasia, held in Melbourne (Australia). The long-term aim of the team was to advance the technology of electric vehicles so that they may become cheaper and more efficient compared to gasoline-powered conventional cars. The car design was finalized after rigorous simulation and analysis of mechanical and electrical systems using software like Ansys, SolidWorks, ADAMS, MATLAB, Lapsim, and Multisim. After all this hard work and patience, the team finally got its reward, the number one Indian team in the Formula Student electric rankings.

    • ACHIEVEMENTS

          FSAE Australasia 2015
  • - 2nd in Cost
    - 4th in Design
    - 4th in Business Presentation
    - 4th position Overall
    - Winner of the ‘CAMS Inspiring Motorsports Award’ from CAMS(Confederation of Australian Motorsports) for Best Design       among first-year electric teams.
    - IIT Roorkee Motorsports became the highest-ranked Formula Student Electric Team in India after this competition till 2019.

    • TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

  • - Top Speed: 100 kmph
    - Acceleration: 0-60 kmph in 7.6 sec
    - Range: 35-40 km
    - Type of cells: Lithium Yttrium polymer
    - Battery Specs: 78.6 V, 8.6 kWh, 90Ah
    - Peak Motor Power: 30 kW
    - Peak Motor Torque:90 Nm
    - Weight (without driver): 240 kg
    - Turning Radius: 1.2m
    - Wheelbase: 1550 mm
    - Frame/Chassis: AISI 1080 TIG-welded tubular frame
